Description

This form can be used to submit requests for changes to Nebula and Exchange accounts:

  • Account creation
  • Changes to account access
  • Account deletion

When the form is submitted it will create a support ticket and send you an email confirmation of your request. Please make note of the ticket number, and refer to it in any future correspondence.

Please allow one to two days to process your request. If you have questions, you can respond to your confirmation email or call (206) 221-5000.
